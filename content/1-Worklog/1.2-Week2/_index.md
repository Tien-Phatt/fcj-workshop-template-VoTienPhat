### Week 2 Objectives:

* Understand the architecture of AWS networking and the core components of Amazon VPC.
* Learn how to design and configure a basic virtual network on AWS.
* Understand how Internet connectivity and communication between AWS resources are established.
* Gain hands-on experience deploying a basic networking infrastructure on AWS.
