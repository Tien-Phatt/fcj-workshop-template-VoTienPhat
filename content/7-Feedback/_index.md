### Overall Evaluation

1. **Working Environment**

The working environment at the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ) Bootcamp** was highly professional, friendly, and collaborative. Mentors and team members were always willing to provide guidance and support whenever I encountered difficulties, creating an environment where interns could continuously learn and grow. The positive working atmosphere kept me motivated to explore new technologies and complete my assigned tasks. In addition, organizing more networking activities or knowledge-sharing sessions among project teams would further strengthen collaboration and connections between participants.

2. **Support from Mentors and Team Admin**

Throughout my internship, my mentor closely monitored my progress, provided detailed guidance, and patiently answered my questions. Instead of simply providing solutions, my mentor encouraged me to think critically and find solutions independently, which greatly improved my problem-solving skills. The Team Admin also offered excellent support by providing documentation, timely announcements, and organizing Bootcamp activities, ensuring that the internship experience ran smoothly.

3. **Relevance of the Work to My Major**

The tasks assigned to me were closely aligned with my major in Information Technology, particularly in the fields of Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence. Through the AI AWS Architecture Reviewer project, I had the opportunity to apply my AWS knowledge, develop serverless applications, and integrate Generative AI into a real-world solution. This experience helped me gain a deeper understanding of the software development lifecycle and how academic knowledge can be applied in practice.

4. **Learning Opportunities and Skill Development**

The Bootcamp provided valuable opportunities to work with modern cloud technologies, including Amazon Bedrock, AWS Lambda, Amazon API Gateway, Amazon S3, AWS Step Functions, and Amazon EventBridge. In addition to technical knowledge, I strengthened essential professional skills such as teamwork, time management, technical documentation, report writing, researching English technical materials, and presenting project outcomes. These skills will be highly valuable in my future career.

5. **Culture and Teamwork**

The culture at FCAJ promotes collaboration, respect, and continuous learning. Team members were always willing to share their experiences, support one another, and work together to overcome challenges during the project. This positive environment helped me become more confident in teamwork and allowed me to learn from experienced mentors and fellow participants.

6. **Internship Program**

The Bootcamp followed a well-structured learning roadmap that effectively combined theoretical training with hands-on experience through real-world projects. I highly appreciated the opportunity to work with modern cloud technologies while receiving continuous guidance from experienced mentors. It is an excellent environment for students to enhance both their technical knowledge and professional skills before entering the industry.

**Additional Questions**

**What was the most satisfying part of your internship?**

The most rewarding aspect of my internship was having the opportunity to contribute directly to a real-world AI project built on AWS. This experience allowed me to gain a deeper understanding of the software development process, strengthen my technical expertise, and acquire practical experience that cannot be fully obtained through classroom learning alone.

**What do you think could be improved for future interns?**

I believe the Bootcamp could offer more in-depth workshops on system architecture, DevOps, and experience-sharing sessions led by industry professionals. Additionally, conducting regular performance reviews and providing more detailed feedback throughout the internship would help interns better understand their progress and improve more effectively.

**Would you recommend this internship program to your friends? Why?**

Yes. I would highly recommend the First Cloud AI Journey Bootcamp to my friends because it provides students with opportunities to work with modern cloud technologies, participate in real-world projects, and learn from experienced mentors. It is an excellent opportunity to gain practical experience and prepare for a professional career after graduation.

**Suggestions and Future Expectations**

**Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**

I would recommend organizing more technical workshops, seminars, and knowledge-sharing sessions on emerging technologies such as Generative AI, MLOps, DevOps, and AWS system architecture. In addition, increasing networking opportunities between mentors and interns would further enhance knowledge exchange and collaboration.

**Would you like to continue participating in this program in the future?**

Yes. If given the opportunity, I would be happy to participate in future training programs or projects organized by FCAJ. I believe this would allow me to further strengthen my technical expertise, develop practical skills, and gain additional experience in Cloud Computing and Artificial Intelligence.

**Additional Comments**

I would like to express my sincere gratitude to all the mentors, the Team Admin, and everyone at the First Cloud AI Journey Bootcamp for providing me with such a valuable learning experience throughout my internship. This internship has been an important milestone in my professional development, helping me improve both my technical competencies and soft skills while providing clearer career direction. I hope the Bootcamp continues to grow and provide meaningful learning opportunities for future interns.
